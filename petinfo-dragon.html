Creaturetopia

Dragons can be found in stories all over the world in many differnt forms. Dragons can be serpent-like, have two or more legs, have two wings, or have wings and legs.

Some of the items you can use on your dragon include:

  • Scale Wash: Specially designed for large reptiles to help them gleam (increases hygine)
  • Spine File: Keep them uniform and looking their best (increases hygine, agility, and endurance)
  • Weights: Keep your dragon fit (increases stregth)
  • Fang Brush: Let's see that beautiful smile! (increases hygine and intelligence

Stats that can help Dragons do well in shows include:

  • Endurance
  • Strength
  • Agility
  • Intelligence
  • Hygine
